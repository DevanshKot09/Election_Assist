Election 2024

Your Voting Journey

A simple, step-by-step guide to ensure your voice is heard. Follow these instructions to prepare for and participate in the upcoming election.

1

Register to vote

The first step to civic participation is ensuring you are on the official voter rolls. Check your status or register online in minutes.

2

Choose your method

Decide how you'll cast your ballot. You can choose between Mail-in, Early Voting, or traditional Election Day polling.

3

Research your ballot

View a sample ballot to understand the candidates and local measures. Informed voting leads to a stronger community.

4

Cast your vote

Whether by mail or in person, ensure your ballot is submitted by the deadline. Find your nearest drop box or polling site.

Deep Dive: Step 2 — Methods

mail

Mail-in Ballot

Request your ballot at least 30 days before election day. Mail it back or use an official secure drop box.

calendar_month

Early In-Person

Avoid the crowds by voting during the two-week window before Election Day. Flexible hours including weekends.

how_to_vote

Election Day

Cast your vote on the first Tuesday of November at your assigned precinct between 7:00 AM and 8:00 PM.
